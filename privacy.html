Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-11

The short version

This site does not use cookies. It does not run analytics (yet). It does not sell or share your data. If you subscribe, your email goes to Formspree and eventually to whatever email platform is used. That's the entire data footprint.

What data is collected

  • Email address — only if you subscribe via the subscribe form. Submitted via Formspree (formspree.io). Stored by Formspree per their privacy policy.
  • Server logs — GitHub Pages logs standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, timestamp, path, user agent). This is infrastructure-level, not tracking. GitHub's privacy policy applies.

No cookies are set. No fingerprinting occurs. No third-party pixels or trackers are loaded.

Analytics

No analytics are currently active on this site. If Google Analytics (GA4) is added in the future, this policy will be updated and a cookie consent banner will be displayed before any tracking begins.

Third-party services

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — handles email form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — serves GSAP and Three.js scripts. jsDelivr's privacy policy applies to CDN requests.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — serves typefaces. Google's privacy policy applies to font requests.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this site. GitHub's privacy policy applies.

Your rights

If you subscribed and want your email removed, reply to any newsletter email with "unsubscribe" in the subject. I'll remove it manually within 48 hours. You can also email directly from the contact info below.
